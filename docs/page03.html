Lung Cancer Precautions & Measures

Precautions:

  • Avoid smoking and exposure to secondhand smoke.
  • Avoid exposure to pollutants such as asbestos, radon, and air pollution.
  • Maintain a healthy diet and exercise regularly.
  • Get regular check-ups and screenings for early detection.

Measures:

  • Quit smoking if you smoke, and avoid starting if you don't.
  • Wear protective gear in hazardous environments.
  • Avoid prolonged exposure to pollutants by using air purifiers and masks.
  • Stay informed about lung cancer risks and symptoms.
